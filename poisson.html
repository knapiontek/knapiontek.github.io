Numeric Example 1-Dimention

\frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = e^{-x^2}(4x^2 - 2)

Integration by parts

\int \frac{du}{dx}v = uv-\int u\frac{dv}{dx}

Double Integral

\frac{dy}{dx} = \int e^{-x^2}(4x^2-2)dx

Let's substitute

\begin{cases} u=e^{-x^2} \Rightarrow \frac{du}{dx} = -2xe^{-x^2} \Rightarrow du=-2xe^{-x^2}dx \\ v=-2x \end{cases}
\begin{aligned} \frac{dy}{dx} &= 4\int x^2e^{-x^2} - 2\int e^{-x^2}dx \\ &= -2xe^{-x^2} + 2\int e^{-x^2}dx - 2\int e^{-x^2}dx \\ &= -2xe^{-x^2} \end{aligned}

Second integral

\begin{matrix} y = \int -2xe^{-x^2}dx & c_1 = 0 \\ \\ u=-x^2 \Rightarrow du=-2xdx \\ \\ y = e^{-x^2} + c_2 \\ \\ y = e^{-x^2} & c_2 = 0 \end{matrix}
